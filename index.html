Understanding Lung Cancer

Lung cancer is one of the most common and life-threatening diseases worldwide. It often remains undetected in early stages due to mild or no symptoms, making treatment more challenging when diagnosed late.Early detection is critical for increasing survival rates.

How Our AI Model Helps

Our AI-driven lung cancer detection system leverages deep learning models to analyze chest X-ray images and detect patterns that indicate the presence of cancerous cells. The model is trained to provide fast, accurate, and non-invasive screening, assisting medical professionals in early-stage diagnosis.

Who Should Get Tested?

Individuals with a history of smoking, persistent cough, shortness of breath, or frequent respiratory infections should undergo regular screenings. Our AI-powered tool helps with preliminary detection, but a medical consultation is always advised for confirmation.
